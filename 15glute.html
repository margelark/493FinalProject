15 Minute Glute Burn


Time: 15 Minutes   Level: Beginner   Equipment necessary: Optional

Phase 1: Warm Up


    Incline walk 5 minutes (treadmill speed 3.3 incline 12)


Phase 2: Get moving


    Standing glute kickback (10-12 reps per side)


    Hip thrust (10-12 reps)


    Goodmornings (10-12 reps)


Phase 3: Glute Burn


    Banded glute bridge (10-12 reps)


    Clam shell (10-12 reps per side)


    Step touch (10-12 reps)


Phase 4: Cool Down


    Curtsy lunge to squat (10-12 per side)


    Frog pump


Phase 5: Stretch!
