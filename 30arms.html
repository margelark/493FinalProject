30 Minute Bicep Burner


Time: 30 Minutes   Level: Intermediate   Equipment necessary: Yes

Phase 1: Warm Up


   Rowing machine (5 minutes)


   High plank (30 seconds)


   Press up (15 reps)


Phase 2: Strength Builder (3-4 sets of 10-12 reps each)


    Dumbbell chest press


    Dumbbell shoulder press


    Lat pull-down


    Seated row


Phase 3: Final Push


    Cable bicep curls (3-4 sets of 10-12 reps)


    Cable triceps push-downs


    Repeat phase 3


Phase 4: Stretch!
