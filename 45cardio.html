45 Minute Cardio


Time: 45 Minutes   Level: Beginner   Equipment necessary: No

Phase 1: Warm Up


   Light jog or brisk walk for 10 minutes.


Phase 2: Calorie Burn


    Run for one minute, 8/10 effort


    Recover: walk or jog for two minutes


    Repeat 10 times


Phase 3: Cool Down


    Cool down with a light jog or brisk walk for 5 minutes


Phase 4: Stretch!
