30 Minute HIIT


Time: 30 Minutes   Level: Advanced   Equipment necessary: No

Phase 1: Cardio Warm Up


   5 minute run


    Jumping jacks (1 minute)


    Inchworms (1 minute)


    Plank (1 minute)


Phase 2: Legs


    Criss cross jumping jacks (30 seconds)


    Squats (30 seconds)


Phase 3: Arms


    Inchworms (30 seconds)


    Push ups (30 seconds)


Phase 4: Abs


    High knees (30 seconds)


    Russian twists (30 seconds)


Phase 5: Final Push


    Fast feet (30 seconds)


    Half burpees (30 seconds)


    Repeat phase 5, 5 times


Phase 6: Rest and Repeat!


    Rest for 1 minute


    Repeat phases 2-5, 5 times


Phase 7: Stretch!
