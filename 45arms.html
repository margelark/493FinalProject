45 Minute Upper Body Burn


Time: 45 Minutes   Level: Advanced   Equipment necessary: Yes

Phase 1: Cardio Warm Up


   6 minute jog


Phase 2: Weights (4 sets of 12 reps)


    EX bar curls


    Dumbbell curls


    Dumbbell tricep extensions


Phase 3: Cardio Break


    8 minute stair master


Phase 4: Weights (4 sets of 12 reps)


    One arm cable curls


    One arm cable tricep extensions


    Cable curls


   Tricep pull downs


Phase 5: Abs (3 rounds total)


    30 second high plank


    30 second elbow plank


    15 second right hand left foot off ground


    15 second left hand right foot off ground


   Press up (15 reps)


Phase 6: Stretch!
