45 Minute Quad Crusher


Time: 45 Minutes   Level: Intermediate   Equipment necessary: Yes

Phase 1: Warm Up (3 sets of each)


   Barbell front squats (8 reps)


   Dumbbell walking lunges (10 reps per leg)


Phase 2: Glute Burn (3 sets of each)


    Barbell hip thrusts (10 reps)


    Bulgarian split squats (12 reps per leg)


Phase 3: Final Push (3 sets of each)


    Single-leg leg extensions (8 reps per leg)


    Hamstring curls (12 reps)


Phase 4: Stretch!
