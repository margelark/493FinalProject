30 Minute Leg Day


Time: 30 Minutes   Level: Beginner   Equipment necessary: Yes

Phase 1: Warm Up


   Leg presses (20 reps)


   Leg extensions with pauses (20 reps)


   Rest for 30 seconds between sets


Phase 2: Build Muscle (3 sets of each)


    Squats or smith machine (12-15 reps)


    Hamstring curls (12-15 reps)


Phase 3: Final Push (3 sets of each)


    Walking lunges (15 down, 15 back)


    Seated calf raises (15 reps slow with a pause then 15 little pulses)


Phase 4: Stretch!
